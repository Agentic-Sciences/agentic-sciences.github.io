Perpetual Futures and Market Quality

Cryptocurrency Microstructure · Kaiko Tick Data · 13TB · 2017–2023

How do perpetual futures affect spot market quality? Tick-level analysis across Binance, Huobi, OKEx — 5,213 trading days, 13 pairs. Causal identification via Huobi's 2021 contract termination.

Key Finding: Effective spreads are 30× larger than quoted spreads. Perpetual futures listing improves spot liquidity by 15–23%.

Prediction Market Microstructure

Polymarket · Hyperliquid DEX · 2024 US Presidential Election

Microstructure analysis of Polymarket's CLOB-based prediction market. Order flow, liquidity provision, and price formation. Comparative analysis with Hyperliquid DEX.

Key Finding: Spreads tighten as event certainty increases. Liquidity concentrates at 50/50 and drains as probability approaches 0 or 1.

Cross-Market Predictability

Crypto–Equity Linkages · Kaiko + TAQ + WRDS · 126TB · 2017–2023

Can crypto signals predict equity returns? BTC realized volatility from 2,024 days of tick data (mean RV: 73.3%). Dynamic correlations, Granger causality, and spillover effects.

Interim: BTC RV shows regime-dependent correlation with VIX. Crypto order flow leads equity moves by 15–30 min during stress.

LLM-Powered Earnings Call Stock Picker

Gemini LLM · WRDS Transcripts (84K indexed) · CRSP · 2021–2022

Using Gemini to analyze earnings call transcripts, generating Buy/Sell/Hold with confidence scores. Backtesting against CRSP returns. 438 signals from 30/50 dates.

Interim: High-confidence (≥8) Buy signals: +3.11% avg 20-day return. Strong buy bias (206 Buy vs 8 Sell) — calibration needed.

Type 2 Diabetes: A Systems Biology Approach

STRING · ChEMBL · GEO · GROMACS · Cornell BioHPC · 660 cores

Large-scale computational analysis: protein interaction networks (13.7M interactions), gene expression meta-analysis (4 datasets, 256 islet samples), drug-target landscapes (3,552 compounds × 7 targets), and 100ns insulin molecular dynamics.

Key Finding: Disease module Z-score = 22.28 (52.6× over random). IL6 is top hub (degree=299) — inflammation drives T2D. 47 multi-target compounds; CHEMBL509032 dual potent (INSR 20nM + GCK 50nM).
