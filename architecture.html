Data — Cryptocurrency (11TB)

Kaiko Consolidated

Tick-level order book snapshots (up to 5,000 levels) and trades across 64 exchanges. 10 years of spot, perpetual futures, quarterly futures, and options data. Enhanced quality versions (v3/v4) with standardized symbology.

Data — US Equity High-Frequency (26TB)

NYSE TAQ (14TB)

Millisecond trades and quotes for all US-listed equities. NBBO, trade conditions, exchange IDs. The gold standard for microstructure.

NASDAQ ITCH (12TB)

TotalView-ITCH feed (v2–v5). Order-level: add, execute, cancel, replace. Enables queue-position analysis.

Design Philosophy

Stability Over Speed

Run at 80% capacity. Prevent OOM crashes. Every script independently runnable.

Stateful Tech, Stateless Research

Technical details persist. Research judgments start fresh — no confirmation bias.

Honest Replication

All discrepancies with literature are documented transparently, not hidden.

Global Compute Leverage

Each API call rents billions in infrastructure. ~20M× cost-to-infra ratio.
